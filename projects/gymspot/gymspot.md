# GymSpot

## Status

In Progress

## Overview

GymSpot is a fitness-focused application designed to help users organize workouts, explore exercises, and discover gyms in a single platform.

The project combines three core ideas:
- workout tracking
- exercise exploration
- gym discovery

Rather than presenting GymSpot as a finished product, this portfolio entry showcases its product planning, architecture, database design, and implementation roadmap.

## Product Vision

GymSpot is designed as a portfolio-ready fitness application with real-world functionality and scalable architecture.

It aims to centralize tasks that users often manage across separate tools, such as workout notes, exercise references, and gym search.

---

## Architecture

The project follows a feature-based structure combined with lightweight Clean Architecture and MVVM.

Its architecture separates presentation, domain, and data concerns while keeping the initial implementation realistic and scalable.
